Discover if you have an internet addiction.

Internet and PC addiction, a growing concern in today's digital age, has garnered increasing attention as individuals spend more time online, often to the detriment of their daily lives. Online activities, such as gaming, social media, and endless browsing, can become addictive, leading to a range of negative consequences, including impaired academic or work performance, strained relationships, and mental health issues. Recognizing the seriousness of this issue, websites and resources have emerged to help individuals assess and address their online behaviors.

These tests provide valuable insights into the extent of one's addiction and offer guidance on seeking help, promoting a healthier balance between the digital world and the physical one. As the online landscape continues to evolve, it's essential for individuals to be aware of the signs of addiction and to utilize these resources to regain control over their digital habits, fostering a more balanced and fulfilling life.
